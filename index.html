Who am I?

Hi, I’m Neha Soni, a passionate Web Developer with hands-on experience in building modern, responsive, and user-friendly applications. My technical expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, enabling me to work confidently across the full stack.
I also have experience with design tools like Figma and Canva, which allow me to design intuitive user interfaces and engaging visuals that complement my development work. This combination of design and development helps me create applications that are both functional and visually appealing.
I learned web development through self-paced learning from YouTube tutorials, W3Schools documentation, and Udemy courses. This journey has not only strengthened my technical skills but also taught me the importance of discipline, curiosity, and continuous learning.
Beyond technical expertise, I value clear communication, strong problem-solving, and teamwork. These qualities enable me to collaborate effectively, explain ideas clearly, and adapt quickly to challenges.
I am continuously exploring new technologies to grow as a developer while striving to contribute to impactful projects and real-world solutions.
